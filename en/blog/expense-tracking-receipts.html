Why This Matters

Manual expense tracking is tedious and error-prone. Discover how automated receipt processing can streamline your expense management, improve accuracy, and save valuable time.

How It Works

VaultCaddy uses advanced AI and OCR technology to automatically extract data from receipts. Simply take a photo or upload a PDF, and our system will:

  • Extract merchant name, date, total amount, and line items
  • Categorize expenses automatically
  • Organize receipts by date and category
  • Export to Excel or integrate with accounting software

Best Practices

To get the most out of receipt scanning:

  • Scan receipts immediately after purchase
  • Ensure good lighting for photos
  • Keep digital backups of all receipts
  • Review and categorize expenses regularly
