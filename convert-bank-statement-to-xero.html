Frequently Asked Questions

What is Xero CSV format?
Xero CSV format is a standardized file format that Xero accepts for importing bank transactions. It includes fields like Date (YYYY-MM-DD), Type, Description, Payee, Reference, Amount, and Balance. VaultCaddy automatically converts your bank statements to this exact format.
How do I import the CSV file into Xero?
After downloading your converted CSV file from VaultCaddy: 1) Log into your Xero account, 2) Go to Banking ‚Üí Bank Accounts, 3) Select "Import a Statement", 4) Choose your bank account, 5) Upload the CSV file, 6) Xero will automatically match transactions. It's that simple!
