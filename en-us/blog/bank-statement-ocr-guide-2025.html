1. What is Bank Statement OCR?

Bank Statement OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is AI technology that automatically extracts transaction data from bank statement PDFs or images, converting them into structured digital format for accounting software.

Without OCR: You manually type each transaction from a PDF bank statement into Excel/accounting software. Takes 20 minutes per statement.

With OCR: AI reads the PDF, extracts all transactions automatically in 3 seconds. You just review and export.

6. 7 Tips to Improve OCR Accuracy

  1. Use Digital PDFs When Possible
    Download statements directly from online banking instead of scanning paper statements. Digital PDFs have 99% accuracy vs 95% for scans.
  2. High-Quality Scans (300 DPI+)
    If scanning paper statements, use 300 DPI or higher. Low-resolution scans (150 DPI) reduce accuracy to 85-90%.
  3. Good Lighting for Photos
    When using smartphone photos: Use good lighting, avoid shadows, lay statement flat, center in frame. VaultCaddy auto-corrects perspective.
  4. Process Complete Statements
    Upload full statement (all pages) rather than partial pages. VaultCaddy validates balances across pages, improving accuracy.
  5. Consistent Bank Format
    Use same bank statement format consistently. VaultCaddy learns patterns and improves accuracy over time.
  6. Review Low-Confidence Items
    VaultCaddy flags uncertain extractions. Quick review (1-2 minutes) ensures 99%+ accuracy.
  7. Provide Feedback
    Correct any errors you find. VaultCaddy AI learns from corrections, improving future accuracy.
